Cardiology

Show your heart some love

While heart disease continues to be the nation’s No. 1 health problem, an estimated 80% of premature heart attacks and strokes are preventable. Understanding your risk factors is a key strategy for keeping your heart healthy.Our heart care experts offer regular blood pressure screenings and specialty lipid clinics to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of your risk factors and personalized counseling to help you achieve your heart health goals.

Neurology

Strongen your Brain

Care for individuals with diseases that affect the brain and nervous system. We are supported by the highly specialized expertise and resources of Mayo Clinic. Ranked #1 in Neurology and Neurosurgery by U.S. News and World Report, Medicose has more than 100 neurologists and neurosurgeons that specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of more than 500 neurological conditions and diseases.

Diabetes(Endocrinology)

Care, education and support for those with endocrine, hormonal, glandular, diabetes and metabolism disorders.

We teach individuals with diabetes and their families how to live healthier and more independently through self-management training, monitoring, medication tracking, activities, meal planning and education.
